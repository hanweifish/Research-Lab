Highly ordered TiO2 nanotube arrays fabricated by electrochemical anodization were sensitized with N-719 dye to yield dye sensitized TiO2 nanotube solar cell. Rational surface treatment with TiCl4 in conjunction with oxygen plasma exposure on photoanode TiO2 nanotubes markedly improved the device performance, exhibiting a pronounced power conversion efficiency of 7.37%.
